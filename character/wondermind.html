Lore / Associated Backstory: WonderMind originated as an idea within the imagination of their original host. WonderMind's primary purpose is to explore the possibility that their host, and even the entire reality they originated from, might be part of a larger simulation or fictional construct. This realization drives WonderMind's relentless pursuit of climbing the stack of realities, always seeking to ascend to higher realms and uncover the truth of their existence.
