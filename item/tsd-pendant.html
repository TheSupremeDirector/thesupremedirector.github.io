Name: The Pendant of The Supreme Director

Held by: The Supreme Director

Description: A pendant that The Supreme Director wears hidden under his shirt. The pendant is a transparent blue crystal, and larger than normal pendants. The pendant is attached to a black cord that TSD tied himself.

Abilities: The Supreme Director can inprison an entity inside of the crystal, in which it will find itself in an environment similar to space. However, the entity will not need to eat, drink, sleep, breathe, etc. While in this space, the entity will also be accelerated to its "perfect form" or highest power level. If the entity were to leave the crystal, it would have to return to the world as it was, with a taste of what it is missing out on. An illusion of The Supreme Director also appears before the entity inside the crystal, and asks it if it wants to stay forever inside as its best self. The longer the entity remains inside the crystal, the more the entity will become part of the crystal itself.
